Kicking off my Rebel Badge journey
How many badges can I tick off?
6 April 2022
3 min read

Updated 26 April 2022 to include four new badges.

My personalised ads are remarkably accurate at times, and Facebook really did not miss the mark with suggesting the Rebel Badge Book for me. The concept is very simple - 52 merit badges to work towards and collect, just like Scouts/Guides, but for adults.

I was never really into Girlguiding as a child. I was a Brownie for only about a month (Iâ€™m sure my empty sash is somewhere in the attic), but I do remember reading the badge book cover to cover. For some reason, bell ringing is what first comes to mind when I think about that book, not that I was ever going to pursue that. As an adult, I love setting myself personal challenges and trying new things. Having had a look through the Rebel Badges and the clauses to complete for them, I found that some of them were things that I already wanted to do one day.

Case in point: this blog! Iâ€™ve been meaning to set this blog up since I got back from my Landâ€™s End to John oâ€™Groats cycle in August 2021, but things kept getting in the way; mostly my inability to decide what I wanted this blog to look like. The Writer badge has been the nudge I needed to get the blog set up - even if it isn't exactly how I'd like it to look just yet - and start revisiting my cycle trip, as well as transfer some posts from my old study abroad blog.

Rebel Badge Club has a supportive and growing online community. I've seen a few members categorise the badges, so I have decided to do the same, according to the likelihood of me completing the badge. I have a busy couple of months coming up, so I'm likely to not start in earnest until June.

I'm also going to try not to backdate any clauses, with the exception of my Land's End to John o'Groats cycle last year counting towards the challenge aspect of the Explorer badge. I just don't think I'll be able to top it, and have no intention of doing anything that physically demanding and life-consuming for some time.

This is all about trying new things and challenging myself, so I'm going to try to complete as much as I can!
